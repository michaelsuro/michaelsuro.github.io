About

I am a full stack iOS developer and mobile designer that has independently delivered four different applications to the App Store. Prior to building mobile apps I was the Director of Operations at Venga (acquired in 2019 by Booking Holdings Inc.). At Venga I managed a team of operations specialists and worked shoulder to shoulder with developers in a product manager capacity. I began to teach myself how to code so I could become a more effective leader, and on the way bought a $9.99 video course on iOS development that changed everything. On a solo trip to India in 2015 I decided to quit my job and pursue iOS development full time. After nearly a year of teaching myself day and night I released Postable. The following month I was connected to an influencer in the fashion and events space in New York City who had a vision for a networking app, less than a year later we had raised a $200k round of seed financing and released Cinq. As Cinq winded down in the fall of 2019 I worked on a passion project purely for fun called The Cities. Nowadays I’m developing apps as a contractor and supporting my latest project, Flip flashcard maker.
